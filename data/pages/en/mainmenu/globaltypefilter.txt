====== Global Cache Type Filter ======

{{:main_filter_default.png?nolink&100 |}}

With this menu you can filter the caches shown on the live map, nearby list, and when searching by type. Just select the cache type.\\ \\ \\ \\

{{:en:global_cache_type_filter_en.png?direct&400 |}}


<WRAP center round important 40%>
The filter setting will remain active (even if you restart c:geo) until you change or reset it.
</WRAP>

<WRAP center round info 40%>
Please note that you can currently either see all caches or only filter exactly one type of cache. 
</WRAP>

<WRAP center round tip 40%>
The different cache types are explained in the section [[..cacheicons|Geocache Type Icons in c:geo]].
</WRAP>
